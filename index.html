A DAW is an electronic device or software for sound designers and musicians alike. Used for editing, recording, mixing, and creating different audio files, DAWs are found in setups ranging from the smallest of laptops to the most complex of studio systems. There are many different versions to choose from. Some are free, but most come at a hefty price. Acquiring a DAW is usually the first step aspiring producers will take towards their career in audio so it is well worth the investment.
